# Bhagavad Gita

## Core Philosophical Foundations

### 1. The Nature of Self (Atman)
- The soul (Atman) is eternal, indestructible, and cannot be killed
- Physical body is temporary; the inner self is immortal
- Recognizing the difference between body and soul is crucial for inner peace

### 2. Karma Yoga (Path of Selfless Action)
- Perform your duties without attachment to results
- Focus on the action, not the outcome
- Do your best work without ego or expectation of personal gain
- Key principle: "Karmanyevadhikaraste Ma Phalesu Kadachana" (You have a right to perform your prescribed duties, but you are not entitled to the fruits of your actions)

### 3. Three Fundamental Paths to Spiritual Growth
- **Karma Yoga**: Path of selfless action
- **Bhakti Yoga**: Path of devotion and love
- **Jnana Yoga**: Path of knowledge and wisdom

### 4. Mental Equilibrium
- Maintain emotional balance in success and failure
- Develop equanimity towards pleasure and pain
- Control your mind; don't let external circumstances control you

### 5. Key Philosophical Insights
- Everything is transient except the eternal soul
- True wisdom comes from detachment
- Inner peace is more important than external achievements
- Beyond physical and material world, there's a higher consciousness

### 6. Practical Life Principles
- Live with purpose and discipline
- Practice self-control and meditation
- Overcome fear and attachment
- Develop inner strength and resilience

### 7. Understanding Different Types of People
- Four primary human temperaments:
  1. Karma Yogis (Action-oriented)
  2. Bhakti Yogis (Devotion-oriented)
  3. Jnana Yogis (Knowledge-oriented)
  4. Spiritual Seekers

### 8. Supreme Wisdom
- Divine exists in everything
- Universe is a manifestation of divine energy
- Interconnectedness of all beings
- Transcend ego to understand universal consciousness

## Practical Application

### KEY TAKEAWAYS
- Do your best without being attached to results
- Maintain inner peace regardless of external situations
- Understand your true self beyond physical limitations
- Live with purpose, discipline, and compassion
- Develop spiritual intelligence alongside professional skills

### LIFE IMPLEMENTATION
- Practice mindfulness and self-reflection
- Perform duties with full commitment
- Develop emotional intelligence
- Cultivate inner strength and resilience
- Seek balance between material and spiritual growth

## Memorable Quote
"Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self." - Bhagavad Gita
